Holographic Memory
Resolution

A guided journey through body, color, and memory. Based on the work of Brent Baum, this experience gently helps you locate emotions held in the body, find the memories connected to them, and reframe them with compassion.

Duration: 15–25 minutes · Best with headphones

You will be asked to speak or type responses. Find a quiet, private space where you feel safe.
